2. Database tables
   - profiles
   - customers
   - vehicles
   - repair_records
   - invoices
   - referral_codes
   - referral_credits
   - employee_permissions

3. Admin permissions
   - Owner: full control
   - Admin: customers/jobs/invoices

   - Customer: own vehicles/history/payments

4. Payments later
   - PayPal invoice/deposit checkout
   - Payment status saved to customer account
